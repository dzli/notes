Here's a list of key deals reported across sectors this week:

Mobix Labs ([MOBX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MOBX#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "Mobix Labs, Inc.")) shares rose nearly +38% premarket on Thursday as it [signed](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4600636-mobix-labs-soars-on-deal-to-buy-drone-maker-vision-aerial#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews " signed ") a binding letter of intent to acquire Montana-based drone maker Vision Aerial.

Tiderock Companies ([TDRK](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/TDRK#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "Tiderock Companies, Inc.")) on Thursday said it had [acquired](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4600674-tiderock-acquires-arcata-global-enters-us-gold-mining-sector#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews " acquired ") commodities brokerage and exchange platform Arcata Global LLC, marking its entry into the U.S. gold mining sector.

Cloudflare ([NET](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NET#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "Cloudflare, Inc.")) [said](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20540691-cloudflare-acquires-voidzero-to-build-the-future-of-the-ai-native-web#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "said") on Thursday it acquired VoidZero, the company behind the Vite JavaScript build tool, as it seeks to strengthen its platform for AI-assisted application development.

MGM Resorts ([MGM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MGM#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "MGM Resorts International")) jumped 15% in premarket trading on report Barry Diller is set to make an $18 billion bid for the casino giant. The amount includes debt.

NCS Multistage Holding ([NCSM](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/NCSM#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "NCS Multistage Holdings, Inc.")) up 11% pre-market Monday after Weatherford International ([WFRD](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/WFRD#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "Weatherford International plc")) said it agreed to [acquire](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20534318-weatherford-announces-definitive-agreement-to-acquire-ncs-multistage-expanding-completions#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "acquire") the energy technology company in a deal valued at ~$151M, with stockholders electing to receive either Weatherford common shares or a combination of stock and cash.

Marex Group ([MRX](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/MRX#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "Marex Group plc")) on Monday [announced](https://seekingalpha.com/pr/20534359-marex-group-plc-acquires-levmet#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "announced") the acquisition of Levmet, a liquidity provider in physical and derivatives markets across base metals, ferrous metals, energy, and power.

KLX Energy Services ([KLXE](https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/KLXE#source=section%3Amain_content%7Cbutton%3Abody_link%7Cfirst_level_url%3Anews "KLX Energy Services Holdings, Inc.")) on Tuesday said it has acquired all assets of Wolfpack Rentals LLC, a provider of surface rental equipment and services to the oil and gas industry, for $17 million.

Triton Partners is nearing an agreement to buy Flender GmbH in a deal valuing the firm at about €3 billion ($3.5 billion).
